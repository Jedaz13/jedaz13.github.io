Frequently Asked Questions

What does FODMAP stand for?

FODMAP stands for Fermentable Oligosaccharides, Disaccharides, Monosaccharides, and Polyols - types of carbohydrates that can trigger digestive symptoms in sensitive individuals.

How do I know my safe serving size?

Safe servings vary by food and individual tolerance. Start with the "safe" amounts listed and adjust based on your response. Working with a qualified practitioner can help you identify your personal thresholds.

Can I eat high FODMAP foods ever?

Many people can reintroduce some high FODMAP foods after the elimination phase. The key is understanding your specific triggers and tolerance levels.
