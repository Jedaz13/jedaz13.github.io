Can I Eat Almonds with IBS?

Reviewed by Paulina Andrzejewska, MSc Nutrition, Monash Low FODMAP Certified
Quick Answer

Almonds are low FODMAP in small portions (10 nuts). Larger amounts contain more GOS and may trigger symptoms.
