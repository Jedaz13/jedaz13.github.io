Can I Eat Yogurt with IBS?

Reviewed by Paulina Andrzejewska, MSc Nutrition, Monash Low FODMAP Certified
Quick Answer

Lactose-free yogurt is safe. Greek yogurt has less lactose than regular. Small amounts may be tolerated.
