Can I Eat Pasta with IBS?

Reviewed by Rebecca Taylor, Registered Nutritionist (RNutr)
Quick Answer

Wheat pasta is high FODMAP due to fructans. Gluten-free pasta is a safe alternative, or try small portions of wheat pasta to test tolerance.
