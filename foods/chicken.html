Can I Eat Chicken with IBS?

Reviewed by Rebecca Taylor, Registered Nutritionist (RNutr)
Quick Answer

Plain chicken is FODMAP-free and an excellent protein choice for IBS. Avoid breaded or heavily marinated preparations.
